Community and Sector Experience – Custom Solutions

Falconis Solutions Ltd designs and delivers Custom Solutions for membership-based and archive-led organisations, including secure membership platforms, digital archive and records systems, data dashboards, and governance-aligned digital services for associations, Community Interest Companies and not-for-profit bodies.

As part of its business development and marketing activity, Falconis Solutions Ltd has supported a limited number of UK heritage and community organisations operating in regulated environments. This activity enables Falconis Solutions Ltd to demonstrate technical capability, develop sector-specific expertise, and showcase the practical application of its Custom Solutions in real-world operational settings.

Support may include financial sponsorship, discounted services, or technical assistance, provided on a transparent and arm’s-length basis, without obligation, exclusivity, or preferential commercial arrangements. All such activity is undertaken wholly in furtherance of Falconis Solutions Ltd’s trade and commercial objectives.

Where Falconis Solutions Ltd has provided support to organisations with which a director of the company is also involved, those relationships are openly declared and managed by the recipient organisation in accordance with its own governance requirements.

Principal Architect
